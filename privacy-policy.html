Privacy Policy – SFE Spirits

Introduction

SFE Spirits (“we”, “us”, “our”) operates https://sfespirits.co.uk (the “Site”).
We respect your privacy. This Site is a simple informational website and does not collect, store, or process any personal data about its visitors.

No Personal Data Collection

We do not request, collect, or store any personally identifiable information (such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, postal addresses, payment details, IP addresses, or device identifiers) from visitors. There are no registration forms, contact forms, checkout flows, or subscription services on the Site that capture personal data.

Cookies, Tracking, and Analytics

The Site does not use third-party analytics tools (e.g., Google Analytics), tracking pixels, or advertising trackers.

Note: The Site may use a simple client-side age-verification disclaimer for legal compliance purposes.

Third-Party Links

The Site does not include links to any third-party websites.

Security

Because we do not collect personal data, no such data is stored on our systems. Standard website security measures (including HTTPS and server hardening) are used to protect the Site and its content.

Children

The Site is intended for adults only. As we do not collect any personal data, we do not knowingly collect information from children under the legal drinking age.

Changes to This Policy

If our privacy practices change in the future, we will update this page and include a revised “Last updated” date. Any future collection of personal data will be clearly communicated and will require user consent, as required by law.
